Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 14, 2025

At GoStudy!, accessible from gostudy.com, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by GoStudy! and how we use it.

1. Information we collect

The personal information that you are asked to provide, and the reasons why you are asked to provide it, will be made clear to you at the point we ask you to provide your personal information.

  • Account Information (Name, Email)
  • Study Documents (PDFs, Notes) - Encrypted
  • Usage Data (Time spent studying)

2. How we use your files

Crucially: We do not use your private uploads to train our public AI models. Your Biology notes remain yours. We use them solely to generate your summaries and quizzes, after which they are stored in an encrypted state.

3. Third Party Processors

We use Google Firebase for authentication and database services. You can find their privacy policy here.

4. Contact Us

If you have additional questions or require more information about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at support@gostudy.com.
