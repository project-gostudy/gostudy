Common Questions

Is using this considered cheating? expand_more
Absolutely not. GoStudy acts as a digital tutor. It does not generate essays or solve homework problems for the student. It simply quizzes them on material they have already uploaded to ensure they understand it.
How secure is my child's data? expand_more
Data security is our top priority. All uploaded notes are encrypted. We do not sell data to advertisers, and we are fully compliant with student privacy laws.
Does this replace the teacher? expand_more
No. The teacher remains the primary source of knowledge. GoStudy is a "post-class" tool intended to help students retain what was taught in the classroom.
Is it free to try? expand_more
Yes, students can sign up for a free Basic account which allows for limited uploads per week. No credit card is required for the free tier.
